Your privacy matters. We are committed to protecting your personal data and being transparent about how we use it.

1Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

Account Data
  • Name and email address
  • Password (hashed, never stored in plain text)
  • Profile picture
  • Professional bio and skills
  • Resume files
Usage Data
  • Jobs viewed and applied to
  • Search queries and filters used
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Device and browser information

2How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve the JobFinder platform.
  • Match job seekers with relevant job opportunities.
  • Enable employers to review applications.
  • Send service-related communications (application updates, etc.).
  • Ensure platform security and prevent fraud.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3Sharing Your Information

We may share your information with:

  • Employers — when you apply to their jobs, they can see your name, email, skills, and resume.
  • Service providers — third-party vendors who help us operate the platform (hosting, analytics), bound by confidentiality agreements.
  • Law enforcement — when required by applicable law or legal process.

4Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely. We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures including:

  • Bcrypt password hashing — passwords are never stored in plain text.
  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit.
  • CSRF protection on all forms.
  • Regular security reviews.

We retain your data for as long as your account is active, or as required by law. You may request deletion at any time.

5Cookies

We use essential cookies to maintain your session and preferences (e.g., dark mode setting). We do not use third-party tracking cookies.
